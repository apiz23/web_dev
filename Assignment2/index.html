About

Tim Cook

Apple Inc. (Apple) develops, produces, and distributes smartphones, laptops, tablets, wearable technology, accessories, and a variety of connected services. iPhone, Mac, iPad, AirPods, Apple TV, Apple Watch, Beats goods, HomePod, iPod touch, and accessories are just a few of the company's offerings. Apple is the world's largest firm by market capitalization as of June 2022, the fourth-largest personal computer vendor by unit sales, the second-largest producer of mobile phones, and the largest technological business by revenue (totaling US$365.8 billion in 2021).
